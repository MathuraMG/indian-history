Famine had been a recurrent feature of life the Indian sub-continental countries of India, Pakistan and Bangladesh, most famously during British Raj. Famines in India resulted in more than 60 million deaths over the course of the 18th, 19th, and early 20th centuries. The last major famine was the Bengal famine of 1943.
